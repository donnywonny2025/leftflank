# CIRCLE 3: PAC INFRASTRUCTURE LAYER
## Law Firms That Handle Democratic PAC Compliance

These firms sit at the center of the ecosystem. When a new PAC is formed, they are the first call. They know every new dollar entering the Democratic political space.

| Firm | Key Contact | Role | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Elias Law Group** | Marc Elias (founder) | #1 Democratic political law firm | Represents DSCC, DCCC, Senate Majority PAC, HMP, Priorities USA. Every major Dem PAC. elias.law |
| **Perkins Coie** | Brian Svoboda (partner, political law) | Major Dem political law firm | Long-standing Democratic FEC compliance work before Elias split off |
| **Covington & Burling** | Political law practice | Major DC firm | Advises campaigns and PACs on FEC compliance |
| **Holtzman Vogel** | Bipartisan | Political law | Both sides, more Republican-leaning |
| **Steptoe** | Jason Abel (partner) | Campaign finance + political law | Bipartisan |

### KEY INSIGHT:
Elias Law Group is the single most important firm in Democratic political law. Marc Elias represents virtually every major Democratic PAC and Senate campaign. His firm is paid by DSCC, DCCC, Senate Majority PAC, House Majority PAC, and Priorities USA — confirmed in the FEC data above (Elias Law Group appears in both WINSENATE and Priorities USA vendor lists). If you ever want to understand who is standing up a new Democratic PAC, Elias Law Group is the first place to look.

### PRACTICAL USE:
These law firms are not direct outreach targets for production work. But understanding who they are helps you understand the ecosystem. When a new PAC materializes with $30M to spend, Elias Law Group is likely their compliance counsel. The firms and consultants that Elias refers to those PACs are the ones who then hire production shops.
