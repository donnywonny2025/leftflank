## THE MODEL
**Left Flank** (Democratic) and **Deep State Media** (Republican) are boutique political video production shops. The model is Shine Creative — a production shop that political consulting firms hire for overflow capacity. The pitch: fast, reliable, remote production; we know your 2024 FEC spend; we want to be your production partner for the 2026 cycle.

**2026 market:** $10.8B projected total ad spend (AdImpact). Production is 5-10% = $500M–$1B production market.

# CIRCLE 3: PAC INFRASTRUCTURE LAYER

## The Ecosystem Map
When a new PAC materializes with $30M to spend, here is the chain:
1. **PAC compliance attorney** (usually Elias Law Group) files the paperwork
2. **Political consulting firm** is hired to run strategy and media (Circle 1 firms)
3. **Production shop** is hired by the consulting firm (Left Flank)
4. **Media buyer** places the finished spots (Waterfront, Grassroots, Screen Strategies)
