## IDAHO - BACK TO IDAHO ⭐⭐⭐

**Campaign:** Reproductive Freedom & Privacy Act (2026 ballot initiative)  
**Status:** Signature gathering phase (need 70,700 signatures by April 30, 2026)  
**Lead Organization:** Idahoans United for Women & Families  
**Website:** backtoidaho.com  

**What It Does:**
- Restore personal liberty for reproductive healthcare decisions
- Birth control, IVF, regulated abortion access
- "Roe Standard" - decriminalized abortion up to fetal viability
- Would end Idaho's trigger ban

**Contact Info:**
- **Website:** backtoidaho.com has contact form
- **Test Emails:** info@backtoidaho.com, press@backtoidaho.com
- **Social:** Facebook, Instagram, TikTok active
- **Need to find:** Campaign manager, communications director names

**Key Intel:**
- Grassroots volunteer-powered campaign
- Nearly 1,000 volunteers gathering signatures
- Deadline: April 30, 2026 for signatures
- Ballot vote: November 2026

**Strategy:** They're in signature-gathering mode NOW. Will need video content for awareness/education campaign leading up to November vote.
